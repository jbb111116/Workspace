How are position, velocity, and acceleration related?

Position and Velocity

Velocity is just the total displacement or change in position that a particle has traveled over a period of time. dX/dt = V in calculus terms

Velocity and Acceleration

Acceleration is the change in velocity that a particle has experienced over a period of time. dV/dt = A in calculus terms

Position and Acceleration

Acceleration is the double derivative of position. d^2(X)/dt^2 = A in calculus terms
